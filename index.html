Phoenix DAO ‚Ä¢ Revival System

Goal

Revive PulseChain tokens using a standardized on-chain playbook that is predictable, auditable, and aligned with real holders and sustainable liquidity.

How it works
  1. Proposal is submitted to Phoenix DAO with a fixed plan and a public ReviveScore hash.
  2. Voting by staking $PHOENIX with quorum and a refundable bond deposit.
  3. Timelock after approval for public review and plan verification.
  4. Execution via a RescuePlaybook contract with whitelisted calls and locked parameters.
  5. Migration Window for legacy holders to swap with a short vesting to prevent instant dumps.
  6. LP Seeding with locks and scheduled renounce, then close and report.
